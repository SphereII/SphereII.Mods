\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This interface signifies that the entity can receiver orders. 

The orders do not have to come from a player. For example, they could come from a pathing cube. 

The entity does not have to {\itshape obey} the orders. It is only assumed that they will adjust their behavior upon receiving at least one of the possible orders as specified in Entity\+Utilities.\+Orders. 

Entities that implement this interface should also descend from Entity\+Alive, or one of its descendants.
