Detailed Description

This interface signifies that the entity can receiver orders.

The orders do not have to come from a player. For example, they could come from a pathing cube.

The entity does not have to obey the orders. It is only assumed that they will adjust their behavior upon receiving at least one of the possible orders as specified in EntityUtilities.Orders.

Entities that implement this interface should also descend from EntityAlive, or one of its descendants.
